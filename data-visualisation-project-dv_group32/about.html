About This Project

Data Visualisation Project for Swinburne University

Project Brief

The Bureau of Infrastructure and Transport Research Economics (BITRE) has published Australian transport statistics since 1970. In 2023, they began collecting more fine-grained detail about road safety enforcement, including location, age group, arrests, and charges.

Our task is to design and implement an interactive data visualisation that allows users to explore this new level of detail, telling a story about some aspect of the data. This site includes 2 different charts and 1 map-related visualisation, all implementing user interactivity, filtering, and animation.

Data Source: datahub.roadsafety.gov.au
